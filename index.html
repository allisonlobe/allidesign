About Me

Hi! My name is Allison Lobe and I’m 14 years old. I love baking because it’s so fun to mix ingredients, try new recipes, and create tasty treats. It all started when I helped my family decorate cupcakes as a little kid, and now I enjoy making all kinds of desserts, like cakes, cookies, and brownies for others. My favorite part of baking is sharing what I make with others and seeing them smile when they try it. Baking makes me happy, and I love learning new skills and ideas to get better.
